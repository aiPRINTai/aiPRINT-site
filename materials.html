Materials & finishes

Three premium options — each archival and ready to hang. Pick the vibe that fits your space.

Signature Series — Fine Art Canvas

  • Archival cotton/poly blend with rich texture
  • Gallery-wrapped edges, wired to hang
  • Classic, painterly feel; lightweight

Gallery Edition — ChromaLuxe Metal

  • Ultra-vivid dyes infused into aluminum
  • Durable float mount for shadowed “hover” look
  • Sleek, modern, exceptionally crisp

Museum Collection — Acrylic Facemount

  • Photo bonded to acrylic for glass-like depth
  • Polished edges, rigid aluminum backer
  • Luxurious, gallery-grade presence

Care: Canvas

Dust gently with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid direct sunlight and moisture.

Care: Metal

Wipe with microfiber and glass cleaner as needed. Don’t use abrasives.

Care: Acrylic

Use acrylic-safe cleaner and microfiber. Avoid paper towels/chemicals.
