Frequently asked questions

Everything you might want to know about generating your artwork, materials, sizing, shipping, returns, and usage rights. If you don’t see your question, contact us—we’re here to help.

Ordering & pricing

How does the ordering flow work?

Create a preview → choose size & finish → pay via secure Stripe checkout. We produce your piece and ship it ready to hang.

Are your prices fixed?

Yes—each size/medium has a set price shown on the Order section. Taxes and shipping (if applicable) are calculated at checkout.

Do I need an account to order?

No. You can checkout as a guest. Later we may offer accounts to view order history or re-order easily.

Image generation

Can I upload a reference image?

Yes—one reference image is supported today. We use it for direction, while still crafting a unique image tailored to your prompt.

Aspect ratios vs. sizes—how do they relate?

Choose 1:1 (Square), 3:2 (Horizontal), or 2:3 (Vertical) when generating. At checkout you’ll see size options that match that aspect.

Can I see multiple versions before ordering?

Our goal is to get you a polished preview in one pass using your options. If you don’t love it, you can regenerate with new settings.

Materials & quality

What are the three finishes?
  • Signature Series – Fine Art Canvas (gallery-wrapped, classic texture)
  • Gallery Edition – ChromaLuxe Metal (sleek, ultra-vibrant, float-mounted)
  • Museum Collection – Acrylic Facemount (depth, clarity, polished edges)
How large can you print with great quality?

For now we offer sizes up to 36" on the long side. Our pipeline upscales intelligently for crisp, exhibition-quality results.

Are pieces signed?

You can optionally place your artist signature on the preview (5 font styles) so it prints with the art—because this is your piece.

Shipping & returns

When will my order ship?

Most orders ship within 7–14 days depending on material and size. You’ll receive tracking as soon as it leaves the studio.

What if my print arrives damaged?

We’ll reprint or provide store credit—just email photos of the packaging and the damage within 14 days so we can take care of you quickly.

Do you ship outside the U.S.?

Not yet. We’ll announce international shipping when our packaging and carrier partners meet our standards globally.

Usage & rights

What rights do I get with my purchase?

You’re free to display or resell the physical piece. Commercial licensing (e.g., packaging, ads, merchandise) is available on request.

Is my image exclusive to me?

Yes—once ordered, we mark the image exclusive and do not resell or reprint it for others. We safely store it for your future reorders.

Signature & personalization

Can I add my signature to the art?

Yes. Toggle “Show signature on preview,” choose one of five fonts (including three cursive options), and drag the signature into place.

Will the signature print exactly where I place it?

Yes—what you see on the preview is what goes to print.

Payments & security

How do you process payments?

We use Stripe for secure checkout (cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, etc.). aiPRINT.ai never stores your card details.

Is my personal information safe?

We only use your info to fulfill your order and send essential updates. No spam. See our Policies.
