Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about AI generation, print quality, timing, and rights.

How does the AI art process work?

You describe your idea, choose style options, and we generate the artwork. Our system saves a high-resolution master, and we produce a premium print in the size & finish you select.

Can I upload a reference photo?

Yes—reference images are optional and help steer composition and subject details. You can add one in the generator.

What sizes do you recommend?

Square (24×24, 30×30), Horizontal (24×16, 30×20), Vertical (16×24, 20×30) are available now. Larger museum sizes can be added later.

What’s included with “ready to hang”?

Canvas arrives gallery-wrapped; Metal includes a float mount; Acrylic includes an aluminum backer with cleat. Hardware is installed.

How long does it take?

Most orders ship in 7–14 days depending on size and material. You’ll receive tracking the moment it leaves the studio.

Do I own the image? Can I resell the print?

Yes. Your print is exclusive to you—we don’t resell your image. Personal display and resale are allowed. For commercial licensing, contact us.

What if my order arrives damaged?

We’ll reprint or offer store credit. See our Policies for details.

Can I request refinements?

We’re currently focused on a frictionless checkout: what you preview is what we print. Refinements may be offered in the future.
