Policies

We keep it simple and fair so you can order with confidence.

Production & Shipping

Prints ship within 7–14 days of ordering depending on the material and size. You’ll receive tracking once your piece leaves the studio.

Exclusivity

Every ordered image is unique to you—it will never be resold or reprinted for another customer.

Reorders

We securely save your image so you can reorder later in any supported size and finish.

Refunds & Returns

Because each piece is custom-made, we can’t offer refunds. If your order arrives damaged or defective, we’ll reprint or offer store credit—just contact us within 14 days.

Usage Rights

Once purchased, you’re free to display or resell your piece. Commercial use is welcome.

Privacy

We only use your information to fulfill your order. No spam, ever.
