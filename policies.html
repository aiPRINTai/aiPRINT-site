Policies

Clear and simple so you know exactly what to expect.

Production & Shipping

Prints typically ship within 7–14 days of ordering depending on size and material. You’ll receive tracking by email as soon as your piece leaves the studio.

Exclusivity

Every ordered image is unique to you—it will never be resold or reprinted for another customer. Reorders for you are available on request.

Refunds & Returns

Because each piece is custom-made, refunds aren’t available. If your order arrives damaged or defective, we’ll reprint or offer store credit. Please contact us within 14 days of delivery with photos of the issue.

Usage Rights

Once purchased, you’re free to display your art at home or sell the physical print. Commercial licensing of the image itself is available—reach out for terms.

Privacy

We only use your info to fulfill your order—no spam. Your saved artwork exists so you can reorder in the future; we don’t publish or resell it.
