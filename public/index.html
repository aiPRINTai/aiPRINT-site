Behind the brand

A print veteran who fell in love with AI art

I'm Lawrence — I've spent years working with high-end premium print labs, and I started aiPRINT because I was tired of seeing incredible AI-generated artwork printed on the cheapest substrate the lowest bidder would run. Every order on this site crosses my desk before it prints. I review the file, confirm the material is right for the image, and number the Certificate of Authenticity by hand. If it's not something I'd hang in my own home, it doesn't ship.

— Lawrence, Founder · Printed, inspected, and numbered in Florida, USA
