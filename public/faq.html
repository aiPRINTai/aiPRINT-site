Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating your artwork, materials, sizing, shipping, and more. Can't find your answer? Contact us.

The honest questions

Is AI-generated art "real" art?

It's a fair question. Your prompt, your style choices, your composition decisions, and a human curator's final review all shape the artwork. The AI is a tool — like a camera or a paintbrush. The result is a one-of-a-kind piece that exists because you made the creative choices behind it.

Who owns the copyright?

You receive full personal-use rights to your artwork — display it, gift it, photograph it. Commercial resale rights are not included by default. The Certificate of Authenticity documents your unique edition. Each generation is unique to you and is not resold to anyone else.

What if I don't love the print when it arrives?

You see and approve the watermarked preview before you order, so there are no surprises in composition. If your print arrives damaged or doesn't match the approved preview, we replace it free. Because each piece is custom-made, we can't accept buyer's-remorse returns — but our 30-day satisfaction guarantee covers any quality issue.

Is it actually unique, or could someone else generate the same thing?

Even with the identical prompt, the model produces a different result every time — different composition, lighting, and detail. We log your generation with a unique ID printed on your Certificate of Authenticity. Your piece is genuinely one of one.

Can I see what the Certificate of Authenticity looks like?

Yes — every print ships with a numbered Certificate of Authenticity on archival paper, documenting the title, medium, dimensions, edition number, issue date, and a unique fingerprint tied to your order. View a sample certificate →

Creating Your Art

How does the AI generation work?

Describe your vision using the text prompt and style dropdowns. Our AI creates a unique, high-resolution artwork based on your specifications. Each piece is one-of-a-kind and exclusive to you.

Can I regenerate if I don't like the preview?

Yes! Click "Generate another" to create a new version with the same settings, or "Start over" to adjust your prompt and style options. Generate as many times as you need until you're happy.

What are the aspect ratios?

Choose from Square (1:1) for Instagram-ready art, Horizontal (3:2) for landscape scenes, or Vertical (2:3) for portraits. Your chosen ratio determines which print sizes are available.

Can I add my signature?

Yes! Click "Add your signature" under the artwork controls and type your name. Pick a font, color, size, and corner — it'll be tastefully embedded in the final print, making it uniquely yours.

Materials & Quality

What are the three finishes?

Canvas: Classic gallery-wrapped canvas with rich texture and deep colors.

Metal: Vibrant ChromaLuxe aluminum with glossy finish and exceptional depth.

Acrylic: Museum-grade facemount with brilliant clarity and polished edges.

Are prints ready to hang?

Yes! All prints arrive ready to hang with appropriate mounting hardware included. Canvas is gallery-wrapped, metal has float mounts, and acrylic includes standoffs.

How large can I print?

Currently up to 36" on the longest side. Our AI upscales intelligently to ensure crisp, gallery-quality results even at large sizes. Each material offers 5 size options per aspect ratio.

What's included with my order?

Your custom print on premium materials, mounting hardware, protective packaging, and a Certificate of Authenticity confirming this is a unique, limited-edition piece.

Shipping & Returns

How long does production take?

Production takes 3–7 business days depending on material, with shipping typically 3–7 days after that. Most orders arrive within 7–14 days total. We'll keep you updated with tracking every step of the way.

How much does shipping cost?

Low flat rates based on the size of the piece — usually $10, up to $35 on the largest pieces. Every order $99 and over ships free, which covers most of the catalog. The exact amount is shown on the checkout page before you pay — never a surprise.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently shipping within the USA only. International shipping is coming soon — email us at info@aiprint.ai to be added to the waitlist.

What if my print arrives damaged?

Contact us immediately with photos of the damage and packaging. We'll send a replacement at no cost. Our packaging is museum-grade, but accidents happen and we'll make it right.

Can I return or exchange?

Since each piece is custom-made, we can't accept returns for buyer's remorse. However, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee - if you're not happy, contact us and we'll work to make it right.

Pricing & Payment

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on size and material. Canvas is most affordable, metal is mid-range, and acrylic is premium. All prices shown include the custom artwork, production, and mounting hardware.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay through Stripe's secure checkout. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

Is there a bulk discount?

For orders of 5+ prints, contact us for custom pricing. We work with interior designers, businesses, and collectors on large projects.

Do you offer gift cards?

Not yet, but they're on the way. Perfect for the art lover who wants to create their own custom piece. Email us at info@aiprint.ai to be notified when they launch.
