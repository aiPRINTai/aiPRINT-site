Shipping Policy

Production Timeline

All prints are made-to-order with careful attention to quality:

  • • Canvas: 3-5 business days production
  • • Metal: 5-7 business days production
  • • Acrylic: 7-10 business days production

Shipping Times & Costs

  • • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days (Free on orders over $200)
  • • Express Shipping: 1-3 business days (rates at checkout)
  • • International: Currently USA only (expanding soon)

Note: You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships. All prints are carefully packaged in museum-quality materials to ensure safe arrival.

Return & Refund Policy

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our prints. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, contact us within 30 days of delivery. We'll work with you to make it right through replacement or refund.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your print arrives damaged or has a quality issue:

  1. Contact us within 14 days of delivery
  2. Provide photos of the damage and packaging
  3. We'll send a replacement at no cost to you
  4. No need to return the damaged item

Custom Nature of Orders

Since each print is custom-generated and produced specifically for you, we cannot accept returns for "change of mind" or if the AI-generated result doesn't meet expectations. That's why we encourage regenerating previews until you're happy before ordering.

Quality Promise: Every print is inspected before shipping. If there's any issue with print quality, color accuracy, or materials, we'll make it right.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to fulfill your order:

  • • Name and email address
  • • Shipping address
  • • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • • Your artwork preferences and generated images

How We Use Your Information

  • • Process and fulfill your order
  • • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • • Respond to customer service requests
  • • Improve our products and services

Your Rights

You can request to see, update, or delete your personal information at any time. We never sell your data to third parties. Your generated artwork remains exclusively yours.

Security: We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, and we never store your credit card information.
