Why it works as a gift

A real piece of art, not just a download

Anyone can generate an AI image. What makes a gift is the part that comes after.

Founder-reviewed before it prints

Every order crosses Lawrence's desk before the press fires. If it's not something he'd hang in his own home, it doesn't ship. No assembly-line surprises.

Numbered Certificate of Authenticity

Every print ships with a hand-numbered COA documenting the title, medium, dimensions, edition number, and unique generation tied to your order. We never resell your purchased artwork.

Museum-grade materials, ready to hang

Archival canvas, ChromaLuxe metal, or acrylic facemount. Every print includes French-cleat hardware — most hang in under 10 minutes, no framing shop required.
