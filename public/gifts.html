Why it works as a gift

A real piece of art, not just a download

Anyone can generate an AI image. What makes a gift is the part that comes after.

Founder-reviewed before it prints

Every order crosses Lawrence's desk before the press fires. If it's not something he'd hang in his own home, it doesn't ship.

Numbered Certificate of Authenticity

Every print ships with a hand-numbered COA documenting the unique generation tied to your order. We never resell your purchased artwork.

Museum-grade, ready to hang

Archival canvas, ChromaLuxe metal, or acrylic facemount. French-cleat hardware included — most hang in under 10 minutes.
